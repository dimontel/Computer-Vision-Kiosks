Food Connection Project

Did you know that food in good condition is still being thrown out? Meanwhile, organizations (ex. school, charity, restaurant and more) are struggling to keep up with high demands for food.

why?

Food in good condition is still being thrown out in supermarket, Meanwhile, organizations are struggling to keep up with high demands for food.

what?

Food Connection Project is a web-based platform that will connect supermarket chains with organizations facing high demands for food. In the platform, supermarket can sell the food in good condition but still being thrown out to maximize the revenue by offering food to organization. In another side, Organization can gain the lowest market price for food by connecting supermarket.

how?

The project goal is to build a food platform for supermarket and organization to connect each other. The business model is to maximize supermarket's revenue and reduce organization's food cost.
