Illuminate Your Way

A tool that helps students learn more about ways to solve their mental health issues

why?

Stressed post-secondary students don't have a way to let them know whether or not they need counselling.

what?

I designed an interactive internet quiz so that students can go through an calming experience to learn how they can improve their current mental state.

how?

Sheridan's counselling office and counsellors gave me information on what kind of issues students come to them for. They gave me the content I needed to create my thesis project.
