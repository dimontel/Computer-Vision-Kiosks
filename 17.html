SolarCrafts

Solar Crafts is an educational website for kids and parents/teachers to learn the advantage of renewable energy.

why?

Urban sustainability is the idea of a city that can be self-sufficient and be able to power itself with renewable sources of energy. Solar energy is one of the main renewable energy, thus, educating people especially the young age group about the advantage of using solar energy is very important.

what?

This website introduces solar energy through different perspectives and it contains various tutorials of solar powered DIY crafts for users to follow. Users could also share comments and ideas on the community page.

how?

The goal of this project is to educate kids and youth the use of solar energy through handful DIY crafts with parents or teachers.
