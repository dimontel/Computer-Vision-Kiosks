ChangeCME

ChangeCME is a free online tool and portfolio for physicians to track what they learn in lectures, seminars and workshops, to see the impact on their practice.

why?

Physician are required to keep track of their continued medical education (CME). No CME tracking database exists for the millions of physicians who regularly track what they learn.

what?

ChangeCME allows physicians to track their CME and promotes implementing what they have learnt into their practice by encouraging them to make a commitment to change.

how?

Through research, it was clear that much of CME is learnt and tracked but not implemented into practice. Not only does ChangeCME allow physicians to keep track of their CME, but also promotes implementing what they have learnt into their practice.
