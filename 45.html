Connecting businesses with their communities to develop sustainable water management solutions

what?

Blue Business is a non-profit organization that promotes a sustainable water future. Through a series of design challenges specific to each business, post-secondary students can contribute their ideas to developing innovative solutions to water-related challenges. Businesses that reduce their water consumption are recognized through the Blue Business certification.

how?

Through an engaging and honest stop motion video businesses are encouraged to take action, and consider how reducing their water consumption can positively impact the growth of their business.
