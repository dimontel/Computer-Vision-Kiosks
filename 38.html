Shujaat Syed

Remnants

A digital art gallery that will give viewers a new way to experience art.

why?

Despite providing a near limitless scope, displaying artwork in a digital setting has been fairly one-note, compared to it's physical counter-space. You either scroll an endless feed or view artwork that is often disconnected from one another.

what?

Remnants is a digital art show that leverages the strength of a traditional art gallery, with the limitless potential of what virtual spaces can provide.

how?

Speaking to artists, and art lovers, I was able to learn that the tangibility and presence of physical art pieces was sorely missing in digital art platforms. There was a real lack of intimacy with the artwork.
