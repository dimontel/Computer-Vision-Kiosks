Time Shield

Time Shield helps young mothers to manage their time efficiently.

why?

Young mothers constantly struggle with time management. Some days, they are not able to accomplish even one task a day other than taking care of their baby.

what?

They will be less stressful and feel more organizable of their time.

how?

Time Shield helps mothers to manage their time efficiently, planning out their goals and multiple daily task. Mothers also able to join the mommy society, sharing your achievements and communicate with others.
