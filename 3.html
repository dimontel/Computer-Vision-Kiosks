Killer 6 - Making a Safer Highway

A smarter highway.

why?

The death of a teen aged family member highlights serious design flaws present in many high-speed roads.

what?

The result is a redesigned highway system that utilizes smart roundabout placement to alter the flow of traffic in a way that discourages head-on collision.

how?

The goal of the design is the effectively eliminate the possibility of head-on collisions, which are usually fatal.
