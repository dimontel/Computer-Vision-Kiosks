Understanding and preparing for your student debt

why?

The average student debt in Canada is now over $25,000 and it takes an average of 9.5 years for these people to pay off their debt after graduation. My main goal is to find a solution the will benefit students that are coming out of their education with some sort of debt.

what?

This mobile web app will help current students saving money while in school. It will be customized for each student depending on their current financial situation. The app will give you the financial numbers, will track the progress of saved money and show you a decrease in your strained mental health, you may have after you graduate.

how?

What I hope to communicate through my tool is awareness and knowledge about debt. I want future, current, and past students to be able to gain knowledge about their own personal debt and help them see that living with debt can be really difficult. It can have a huge impact on your mental health.
