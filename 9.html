LearnLab

Introducing augmented reality into classroom learning.

why?

Its no secret that there are other ways to learn than reading a textbook, but current standards haven't been updated in years. Education practices are in dire need of modernization. Young students are growing up in a world flourishing with technology, so their education should be able to represent that.

what?

LearnLab is a classroom learning mobile app that uses augmented reality technology to help students understand concepts that are difficult to understand in a textbook. Using modern technology, LearnLab appeals to students and captivates their attention all while incorporating a quiz style approach to helping students learn.

how?

After months of research and interviews with high schoolers and teachers, I found that students are spending an average of 4 hours every day memorizing information read in their textbooks, and students hate textbooks. After finding this, I looked to find a way to modernize how teachers approach learning styles.
