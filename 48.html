A tutorial for beginner of Go (Board Game)

A tutorial of Go (Board Game) for beginners. Helps beginners to have a enjoyable experience learning Go.

why?

Go is a very popular board game that is easy to learn but hard to master. There are many players have difficulty to improve their skill in go. This made some players' game less enjoyable.

what?

This app starts from explaining the basic rule of Go. After that, many courses are provided for different levels of players. There are also quiz and progression system to help players keep progressing.

how?

After talking to many different go players. I decided to make an app to help players to learn about go.
