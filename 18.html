Sous Chef

A grocery list is created by simply selecting recipes you plan on cooking.

why?

Food waste is an issue all around the world. Most people think that the culprit to food waste are grocery stores, but in reality, roughly 51% of food waste is thrown out at home. Most of that food is perfectly edible. I'm guilty of this too.

what?

In the end, I created a mobile app that generates a grocery list based on recipes you want to make. The goal is to help get people more organized and only buy what is necessary.

how?

I found out very quickly that changing people's behavioural habits can be tricky and that finding one solution to fix the whole problem will be almost impossible. Because of this, I chose to focus on and improve one part of the grocery shopping experience.
