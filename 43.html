Robert Whittaker

First Appearence

Unable bring your comics with you to read on the go, can't find that elusive out of print comic, or perhaps you simply lack the funds for physical comics, well here is your answer.

why?

I pursued this project at first as a more personal interest. I have always been a voracious reader, weather it is comics, books, or graphic novels. However, books or comics tend to accumulate in cost over time and older or rare comics even more so, going for thousands of dollars.

what?

The Application will provide users with both an affordable alternative to purchasing older or rare comics while eliminating the need to bring the physical reading material with them. This essentially allows users to access normally out of print content directly form the apps servers minus the exuberant costs.

how?

The project will function as a mobile platform, allowing users to purchase and view digital comics. The app will focus on the accessibility of older, out of print or comics that would normally fetch obscene amounts of money but as a digital copy is a mere fraction of the cost.
