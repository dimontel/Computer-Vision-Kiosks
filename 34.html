Aaron Redublo

Spend Time

An interactive film which follows three perspectives simultaneously.

why?

With the constant advancements in technology, films have the opportunity to introduce a level of interactivity to the viewer. I wanted to develop an experience in the spirit of non-linear storytelling, which gives viewers the ability to jump through multiple perspectives at any point during the runtime.

what?

An interactive film, which follows three perspectives simultaneously.

how?

I first developed the story as three parallel perspectives that interconnect, then conducted user tests to get people's feedback on the experience, and developed a number of ways to present it.
