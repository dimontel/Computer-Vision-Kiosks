Encounter

Improving the quality of interaction between online dating users.

why?

This mobile app will enable interaction between strangers, with ease in a physical environment. The app will provide the user with a informative page about their match, and generate a icebreaker questionnaire based on their match's interests and hobbies, to encourage further interactivity. These features will relax a user and decrease existing pretensions.

what?

Online dating is a popular communication platform that enables strangers to find new personal connections, but can be intimidating to use for many individuals. I gathered multiple perspectives from users regarding their interaction with strangers, in which many responded that they often lose a sense of direction in the conversation.

how?

Based on the user research, many users said that they often lose a sense of direction in the conversation. Therefore, the goal is to improve the quality of interaction between online dating users. This includes eliminating miscommunication and decrease awkwardness or pretensions when talking to a stranger.
