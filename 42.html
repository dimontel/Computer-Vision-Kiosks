Kendall Warnock

Flux Education

Helping transition new teachers into the classroom by providing them with informative resources and engaging activities.

why?

It’s not uncommon for a new teacher to feel nervous, not well supported, and unprepared when entering the classroom for the first time.

Too many resources located in too many places, getting to know your students, and let alone adapting to a new environment. How are we meant to expect teachers to grasp all of this on top of actually teaching their students 8 hours a day?

what?

Through conversations, value propositions, and usability testing directly with teachers I was capable of stepping into their shoes.

Understanding their perspective on what it’s like as a teacher adapting into the world of teaching, and how overwhelming it actually is. This brought me to learning about their key pain points and conceptualizing solutions to what it was they really needed.

how?

Flux Education enables teachers to save time, enhance classroom culture, and allow them to share their knowledge with the greater teaching community. This digital platform introduces a new way to lesson plan, access activities, and contribute.

Teachers can avoid scrambling between PDFs, booklets and websites and find everything they need in one space. The end goal is to make teachers feel confident and prepared walking into the classroom.
