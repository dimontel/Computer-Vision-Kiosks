Michael Gingras

Constant

With the average student carrying over $25,000 in education loans, they need a tool to help stay on top of it.

why?

The average debt-ridden student carries over $25,000 in student loans. It’s a confusing and stressful long-term commitment that can set back major milestones, such as buying a home, or starting a family. It is important to understand debt and it’s complexities in order to pay it off effectively.

what?

Constant is a mobile app that breaks down the intimidating amount of students’ debt into a manageable plan. It allows a recent grad to build a plan around their needs and goals. They’ll have confidence in the influencing factors of their debt and how to manage it most effectively.

how?

I spoke with current students and recent graduates about their concerns towards debt. I learned that students found their debt confusing, and felt their total debt was intimidating. This led the design of Constant, which is focused on giving the individual increased control over their debt.
